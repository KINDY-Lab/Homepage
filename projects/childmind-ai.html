Abstract

ChildMind AI Education (童心智教) is China's first deployed LLM-based instructional quality assessment system for early childhood education. The system leverages large language models to provide expert-level, scalable classroom observation coding — transforming how kindergarten teaching quality is evaluated across China. Currently scaling to 65 kindergartens and 5,000+ children across all 9 districts of Shenzhen in partnership with the Shenzhen Education Supervision Center (深圳市教育督导评估中心).

The Challenge

Why Conventional Evaluation Doesn't Scale

⏱️

Time-Intensive

Expert observers require extensive training and hours per classroom session.

💰

Costly

Hiring trained evaluators at scale is prohibitively expensive for most districts.

📊

Subjective

Human coding varies between raters, limiting reliability of quality assessments.

Our Solution

How ChildMind AI Works

ChildMind AI uses fine-tuned large language models to analyze classroom transcripts and video recordings, automatically coding instructional quality using validated frameworks such as CLASS and ECERS. The system provides real-time feedback and detailed quality reports.
