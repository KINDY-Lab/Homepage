About KINDY Lab

Where Developmental Science Meets Real-World Impact

KINDY Lab investigates the science of early childhood education — how classroom quality shapes children's cognitive, social-emotional, and behavioral development, and how targeted interventions can close opportunity gaps from the very start of life.

"Every child deserves a high-quality early education. Our role is to build the science — and the technology — that makes that possible at scale."
Dr. Runke Huang (黄润珂)
Assistant Professor · CUHK Shenzhen
PhD · Oxford MEd · HKU BEd · BNU
